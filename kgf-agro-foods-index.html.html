50 TCD plant specs

Technical specifications you can keep on the site.

Core process line

  • 14” × 11” 6-roller mill with drive motors and planetary system.
  • Approx. 19–20 ft cane carrier.
  • Juice clarification and heating system in MS / SS304 configuration.
  • Clarifier and crystallizer pans in the 900–1400 litre range.

Thermic fluid boiler system

  • 15 LKCal vertical thermic fluid heater setup.
  • Radiant and convective multi-pass heat exchangers.
  • Furnace with nozzle bed, APH, FD fan, ID fan, dust collector, oil pump and expansion tank.
  • Automatic boiler control panel and temperature-linked bagasse feeding.

Utilities and controls

  • Oil pipelines, control valves, temperature displays, and pan-wise controls.
  • Juice pump around 260 LPM and 1500-litre juice tank.
  • Electrical cables, MCBs, alarms, indicators, and separate control panels.
  • Staging platform and plant access structure included in machinery scope.

Quoted commercial options

  • 50 TCD SS304 3-pan system: ₹149.60 lakhs + GST.
  • 50 TCD SS304 2-pan system: ₹137.50 lakhs + GST.
  • Delivery after receipt of techno-commercially clear order and 60% advance: 3 to 4 months.
  • Erection and commissioning window: 1 month from receipt of material at site. [file:212]
