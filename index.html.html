Our Story

Rooted in Aland.
Built for India.

KGF Agro Food Industries Pvt. Ltd. is a family-owned jaggery manufacturing company established in Bhusnoor village, Aland Taluk, Kalaburagi District, Karnataka. Built on a 10-acre ancestral farm, our 50 TCD automatic plant uses a modern thermic fluid heating system to produce pure, chemical-free jaggery that meets FSSAI quality standards.

Founded on the vision of former CAPF veteran Mr. Siddanna Pujari, KGF Agro Foods bridges traditional jaggery-making wisdom with modern food processing technology.

How We Make It

From Field to Mould —
5 Careful Steps.

Our fully automatic plant follows a controlled, hygienic 5-stage process that preserves the natural goodness of sugarcane while meeting food safety standards.

  1. 1

    🌾 Cane Sourcing

    Fresh sugarcane sourced directly from Aland-area farmers. 50 tonnes crushed daily during the 150-day season, ensuring freshness and traceability.

    50 MT/day · Aland Farmers
  2. 2

    ⚙️ Crushing

    14"×11" six-roller mill with planetary drive and 19–20 ft cane carrier. Maximum juice extraction with minimum energy, powered by Padma Engineering's precision system.

    6-Roller Mill · Planetary Drive
  3. 3

    🔥 Clarification & Heating

    SS304 thermic fluid heating system at precisely controlled temperatures — no open flames, no smoke contamination. Juice clarified with 900–1,400 litre pans for consistent colour and Brix.

    15 LKCal Boiler · SS304 Pans · No Open Flame
  4. 4

    🍯 Moulding & Cooling

    Jaggery poured into standardised moulds, air-cooled under hygienic conditions, and quality-checked for Brix, moisture, and colour grade before release.

    QC Checked · Brix & Moisture Test
  5. 5

    📦 Packing & Dispatch

    FSSAI-labelled, food-grade packed in 500g, 1kg, 5kg, or bulk options. Dispatched to APMC Gulbarga, Bidar, and direct B2B buyers across Karnataka.

    FSSAI Labelled · APMC Approved
